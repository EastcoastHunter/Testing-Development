Services

At Ambient Cooling & Heating, we offer honest, trustworthy, knowledgeable, and reliable service to all of our residential and commercial customers. We look forward to making your home a warm (or cool!) place to be as your preferred HVAC contractor. Here are some of the expert services that our technicians provide.

Residential Services

We offer a wide range of services including residential heating services, residential cooling services, heat pump services, HVAC repair services, boiler services, furnace services, geothermal services, and residential HVAC maintenance plans.

Commercial Services

As a small business, we know that commercial properties value reliability, expertly done work, and prompt service. We offer a variety of commercial heating services and commercial cooling services including rooftop unit installation services, HVAC repair services, and rooftop unit repairs. We look forward to keeping you, your employees, and your customers comfortable.

Heating Services

During the cold winter months in Maryland, keeping your furnace, heat pump, geothermal unit, or boiler working properly is incredibly important. Expertly done scheduled preventative maintenance, installation, and repairs from Ambient Cooling & Heating will ensure that your heating system won’t let you down on cold winter nights. We always show up on time and provide honest and knowledgeable residential heating repairs and heating installation services.

Cooling Services

Throughout the warm and humid summer months, your air conditioner keeps you and your family comfortable and cool. Taking proper care of your residential or commercial cooling system will ensure that it continues to serve you and your property. Ambient Cooling & Heating offers cooling installation services, residential cooling repairs, commercial cooling services, and residential HVAC maintenance plans to keep your cooling systems in great shape every month of the year.

Heat Pumps

Heat pumps are one of the best ways to keep your property warm during the frigid Maryland winters and cool during the hot summers. Ambient Cooling & Heating provides all of our customers with heat pump services and heat pump repairs in addition to scheduled preventative maintenance. We can walk you through each step of the installation process and provide our honest expertise if any issues arise.

Boilers

Boilers are used throughout many homes and apartment buildings to keep rooms warm and cozy all winter long. At Ambient Cooling & Heating, we offer all of our customers professional and knowledgeable boiler installation, boiler repairs, and scheduled preventative boiler maintenance.

Furnaces

Your furnace makes all of the difference during frigid winter months. Ambient Cooling & Heating offers a whole range of furnace services to all of our customers, including installation, repairs, and maintenance. All of our technicians are knowledgeable, professional, and honest.

Geothermal Units

If you’re looking for a sustainable, phenomenally energy efficient, and effective heating and cooling solution for your home or business, geothermal units are the choice for you. Ambient Cooling & Heating stocks a variety of geothermal heat pumps to keep your home warm in the winter and cool in the summer. In addition to our installation services, we offer geothermal unit repairs and scheduled preventative maintenance.

Rooftop Units

Whether you own a residential or commercial property, Ambient Cooling & Heating offers rooftop unit installation and rooftop unit repairs. We have years of experience in installing and maintaining rooftop units and we’re proud to offer quick, reliable, and honest service to all of our residential and commercial customers.

Service Repairs

We know that it can be stressful waiting for a repair technician to show up, so we always show up on time and work hard to get your residential cooling repairs, heat pump repairs, furnace repairs, geothermal unit repairs, and commercial heating and cooling repairs done as quickly as possible.

Heating and Cooling Systems from Ambient Cooling & Heating

If you need quality, expertise, and service that you can trust, choose Ambient Cooling & Heating for all of your heating and cooling contractor needs. We’re known throughout the state of Maryland for our top-notch service and excellent response times and work throughout Baltimore, Anne Arundel County, Howard County, Carroll County, and the lower portion of Baltimore County. We take pride in offering our residential and commercial customers high quality services at competitive price points. Trust a small, family-owned business with over 50 years of combined experience for your heating and cooling system needs. Give us a call at 410-761-0932 or contact us today!
